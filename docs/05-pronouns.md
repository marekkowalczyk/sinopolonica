## Personal Pronouns

Each personal pronoun receives a character. Suppletive stems are absorbed
per P09. Redundant stem material is dropped per P10 — the ending alone
disambiguates.

### Base characters

| Character | Polish (NOM) | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 吾 | ja | I |
| 汝 | ty | you (sg.) |
| 彼 | on / ona / ono | he / she / it |

### Declension of 吾 (ja / I)

| 漢字-Polish | Polish | Case | Absorbed stem |
|---|---|---|---|
| 吾 | ja | NOM | ja- |
| 吾ę | mnie | ACC | mn- |
| 吾ie | mnie / mi | DAT / LOC | mn- / m- |
| 吾ą | mną | INST | mn- |

### Declension of 彼 (on / he)

| 漢字-Polish | Polish | Case | Absorbed stem |
|---|---|---|---|
| 彼 | on | NOM | on- |
| 彼o | go / jego | GEN / ACC | g- / jeg- |
| 彼u | mu / jemu | DAT | m- / jem- |
| 彼m | nim | INST / LOC | ni- |

Note per P10: 彼o (not 彼ego) — the ending -o suffices for GEN/ACC.

## Possessive Pronouns

Possessives are inflected forms of the personal pronoun characters:

| 漢字-Polish | Polish | Analysis |
|---|---|---|
| 吾ój | mój | I-POSS.MASC.NOM |
| 吾a | moja | I-POSS.FEM.NOM |
| 吾e | moje | I-POSS.NEUT.NOM |
| 吾ego | mojego | I-POSS.MASC.GEN |
| 汝ój | twój | you-POSS.MASC.NOM |
| 汝a | twoja | you-POSS.FEM.NOM |

## Reflexive Pronoun and Possessive

| Character | Polish | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 己 | się / siebie | self (reflexive) |

Declension:

| 漢字-Polish | Polish | Case |
|---|---|---|
| 己ę | siebie | ACC |
| 己ie | sobie | DAT / LOC |
| 己ą | sobą | INST |

Reflexive possessive:

| 漢字-Polish | Polish | Analysis |
|---|---|---|
| 己ój | swój | REFL-POSS.MASC |
| 己a | swoja | REFL-POSS.FEM |
| 己e | swoje | REFL-POSS.NEUT |

The distinction 吾ój (mój) vs. 己ój (swój) is visually transparent:
吾 = external "I" reference, 己 = reflexive (subject = possessor). This
disambiguates a distinction that Polish learners struggle with for years.

## Interrogative Pronouns

| Character | Polish | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 誰 | kto | who |
| 何 | co | what |
| 處 | gdzie | where |
| 時 | gdy / kiedy | when |

## Derived Pronominal Forms (P11, P18)

Polish builds entire pronominal series by combining interrogative roots
with prefixes and suffixes. The system decomposes these fossilized forms
to expose their internal logic.

### Negative series (不 + interrogative)

| 漢字-Polish | Polish | Decomposition |
|---|---|---|
| 不誰 | nikt | not + who = nobody |
| 不何 | nic | not + what = nothing |
| 不處 | nigdzie | not + where = nowhere |
| 不時 | nigdy | not + when = never |

### Indefinite series (interrogative + ś)

| 漢字-Polish | Polish | Decomposition |
|---|---|---|
| 誰ś | ktoś | who + INDEF = someone |
| 何ś | coś | what + INDEF = something |
| 處ś | gdzieś | where + INDEF = somewhere |
| 時ś | kiedyś | when + INDEF = sometime |

### Universal series

| 漢字-Polish | Polish | Decomposition |
|---|---|---|
| 皆處 | wszędzie | all + where = everywhere |
| 恆 | zawsze | always (single character) |

## Other Pronoun-Like Words

| Character | Polish | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 獨 | sam | alone, by oneself |
| 此 | ten / ta / to | this (demonstrative) |
| 彼 | tamten | that (distant) |

Demonstrative 此 takes endings: 此ej (tej), 此ym (tym), 此ę (tę).
