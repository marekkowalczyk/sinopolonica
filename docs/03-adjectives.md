## Basic Principle

The character carries the qualitative root. Gender, case, and number
endings attach as Latin suffixes.

## Agreement Paradigm

Example: еҸӨ (stary / old)

| еҸӨa | stara | old-FEM.NOM.SG |

The character еҸӨ remains constant across all 14+ inflected forms. The
Latin tail does all the grammatical work.

Latin script per P04:

## Irregular Comparison

Suppletive comparatives are absorbed per P09:

| жјўеӯ—-Polish | Polish | Note |
|---|---|---|
| е–„y | dobry | good-MASC.NOM |
| е–„szy | lepszy | good-COMP.MASC.NOM |

The stem shift *dobr- вҶ’ lepsz-* is hidden beneath е–„. The reader knows
from the comparative ending that the pronunciation shifts.
